About Me

Hi, I'm Emir McGreevy — a statistics major and UX design minor at the University of Michigan. I combine data analysis and human-centered design to uncover insights and build solutions, especially in the world of sports. I'm passionate about making information intuitive and impactful — whether it's visualizing defensive metrics in baseball or improving athlete training experiences through UX research.

Outside of academics, I am apart of the Michigan Sports Analytics Society, work with Catapult sensor data for the volleyball team, and am a member of Alpha Delta Pi Sorority.
